Quantifly

Warehouse & inventory management, simplified.

Quantifly is a mobile and web app for tracking warehouses, boxes, and items - built for teams who need a fast, reliable way to know what's in stock and where it is.

  • 📦 Organize inventory across multiple warehouses and boxes
  • 🔍 Search items instantly across your entire inventory
  • 📷 Scan QR codes to move and look up boxes and items on the go
  • 🔐 Sign in securely with your Google account

Quantifly is available on iOS and Android, and uses Google Sign-In to authenticate accounts.
